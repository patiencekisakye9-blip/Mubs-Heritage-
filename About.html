Background Information.

The Study Resources Hub was created by Makerere University Business School students. It started as a coursework project but it was later made into something realistic and applicable to make learning materials accessible to all learners, supporting Quality Education.

Our Mission & Vision.

To provide free, reliable,and easily accessible learning resourses to empower students with academic knowledge.

Our Objectives

  1. Provide downloadable past papers and notes.
  2. Provide recordings of online lectures.
  3. Host educational videos for self-paced learning.
  4. Encourage collaboration and academic sharing
