Historical Landmarks at MUBS

Makerere business school mainly known as mubs was established in 1997 and become operational in 1997. It was an idea brought by Prof.BALUNYWA WASSAWA JUMA who played a cructial role in shaping the mighty instution and contributed to its growth and development.MUBs was a result of a mergerof the faculty of commerce at Makerere University and the National College of BUsiness Studies(NCBS).This strategic union positioned MUBS as a leading institution for business education in Uganda.

Variouus branches include:

  • MAIN CAMPUS LOCATED IN NAKAWA DIVISION
  • JINJA BRANCH
  • ARUA BRANCH
  • MBARARA BRANCH
  • MBALE BRANCH

Administration Block

The oldest building at MUBS, symbolizing its establishment and growth. It has hosted countless students and leaders since the school’s founding.

The Library

A hub of learning and research, the MUBS Library stands as a modern monument to academic excellence and innovation.

Main Hall

Venue for cultural events, and conferences that define the academic life of the institution.

Statue
